About Me

Family

I come from Colorado, in a little town known as Holyoke. I have four sisters one of which attended Hastings College as part of the Class of 2015. My Father earned the degree Doctor of Chiropractics from Palmer College of Chiropractics , My Mother has her hands full at home with my younger, more active sister, along with a very happy Golden Retreiver pup named, Gus. Two of my sisters live in Granbury, Texas, working as Boarding House Parents. I am now a student at Hastings College, studing Computer Science and Small Business .

Interest/Hobbies

If I could become a professional movie viewer, I would. Aside from watching movies, I enjoy a casual game of golf with my friends ever so often. I was on my high school team as the fifth Varsity member. This meant that I was the Best JR. Varsity player, but was not good enough to play Varsity. Although I enjoy being outside, I'll gladly spend the entire day inside with people that make me happy. I chose to pursue Computer Science because its a field with demand, I enjoy it, so I dont mind.

School Life

If it is at all possible for me to complete my education in three years, I'll try my best to do so. I wasn't entirely sure how this credit system worked, I signed up for 18 hours. When i told my parents that they responded roughly along the lines of "You won't have a Social Life ." I wasn't to worried about having one anyways. I did spread out my classes as much as I could, I didn't want to get to much on my plate for any one day. I have three classes on Monday, Two on Wednesday, and Friday, and three others on Tuesday and Thursday. I've set a goal to finish in Three years and I don't plan on letting up.
